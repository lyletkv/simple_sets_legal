Simple Sets — Setlist and Songbook Manager

The setlist app built for practice and performance. Organize your songs, build setlists, and perform with confidence — all without an internet connection.

For the Stage

Pull up any song in seconds during a live performance. Lyrics and chords are displayed in a clean, full-screen view optimized for low light. Songs are broken into pages you can navigate with a tap, swipe, or Bluetooth foot pedal — keeping your hands on your instrument the whole time. Auto-scroll handles the scrolling for you when you need it. Need to change keys at soundcheck? Transpose on the fly and save the new key with a single tap.

Chord Support Built In

Import ChordPro files or plain-text lyrics with chords above the words — Simple Sets detects and converts them automatically. Guitar and piano chord diagrams appear inline so you never have to look anything up mid-show.

Your Songs, Your Way

Customize fonts, text size, colors, and line spacing per song. Set a scroll speed for each track. Use dual-view mode to see two verses side by side on a tablet. Print a physical setlist with all of your lyrics or chords in just a couple of taps.

Everything in One Place

  • Song library with search and alphabetical browsing
  • Build setlists by dragging songs into any order
  • Built-in metronome with tap tempo
  • Share individual songs or entire setlists as .simpleset files
  • Full backup and restore — export your library anytime, import on any device
